Hi! I am a senior research economist in the Monetary Analysis Directorate at the Bank of England. My research interests are in macroeconomics, firm dynamics, and monetary policy. My work to date has primarily focused on the interactions between labor markets, firm dynamics and business cycles.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the London School of Economics.
